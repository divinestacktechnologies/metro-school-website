Common Questions

Admissions FAQs

Admissions for the 2026-27 academic session are open now. Submit an enquiry to receive updates on important dates and deadlines.

The process involves submitting an enquiry, a campus visit, an age-appropriate assessment or interaction, document submission and fee payment, followed by an orientation session.

Typically required documents include the child's birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), address proof, and passport-size photographs. Exact requirements will be shared by the admissions desk.

For younger classes, admission involves a simple interaction rather than a formal test. For higher classes, a basic assessment may be conducted.

The fee structure varies by class and includes a one-time admission fee and annual tuition fee. See the fee table above or contact the admissions desk for exact figures.

Transport fee is generally charged separately based on the distance and route. Please confirm with the admissions desk for details.
